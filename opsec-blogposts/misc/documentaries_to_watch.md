# Recommended Documentaries to Watch

Below are some Documentaries that we recommend you to watch. Those are not meant to bring solace, but rather to open your eyes on how dirty the current world we live in actually is:

- **The Creepy Line** - Robert Epstein on the real manipulative power of Google
- **CitizenFour** - Edward Snowden on mass surveillance
- **The Revolution of our times** - The 2021 mass protests in Hong Kong, from the view of the protesters *(warning it's totally unfiltered, it actually shows some protesters getting killed)*
- **We Steal Secrets (2013)** - WikiLeaks documentary
- **The Trust Fall** - Julian Assange and WikiLeaks
- **Silk Road (2021)** - Ross Ulbricht and [Silk Road marketplace](../silk_road/index.md)
- **Running with the Devil The Wild World of John McAfee** - [John McAfee](../lawfareexplained/index.md#the-john-mcaffee-case)'s life
- **Cyberbunker: The Criminal Underworld** - Herman-Johan Xennt and Cyberbunker
- **The Pirate Bay Away From Keyboard** - Covering how the pirate bay got prosecuted for allegedly not respecting copyright laws
- **The Great Hack** - The [Cambridge Analytica](../stateistheenemy/index.md#cambridge-analytica-and-governments-ukus-2010s) scandal
- **The Social Dilemma** - Docudrama focusing on the state of modern [social media](../mediapropaganda/index.md)
